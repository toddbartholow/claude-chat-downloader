Lorem Ipsum has a surprisingly long and interesting history that stretches back to classical antiquity.

Origins in Ancient Rome

The text traces back to 45 BC, to a work by the Roman statesman and philosopher Cicero called De finibus bonorum et malorum ("On the Ends of Good and Evil"). This was a treatise on ethics that explored theories of what is good and what is evil. The Lorem Ipsum text is a scrambled, altered excerpt from Section 1.10.32 of that work.

Medieval and Early Print Use

While its use in the intervening centuries is harder to trace, Lorem Ipsum became a standard in the printing industry during the 1500s. An unknown printer reportedly took a galley of type and scrambled it to make a type specimen book. The fact that it resembles Latin but is mostly nonsensical made it ideal as placeholder text — it has the visual rhythm and distribution of letters found in natural language without distracting readers with meaningful content.

Why It Works

The genius of Lorem Ipsum is that it has a roughly normal distribution of letters and word lengths, so a page filled with it looks like readable text. This makes it far more useful for design and typesetting mockups than simply repeating "content here" or using random characters.

Modern Revival

Lorem Ipsum saw a major resurgence in the 1960s when Letraset, a company that made dry-transfer lettering sheets used by graphic designers, included Lorem Ipsum passages on its sheets. This spread it throughout the design world.

Then in the 1980s and 1990s, desktop publishing software like Aldus PageMaker bundled Lorem Ipsum as default placeholder text, cementing its status as the industry standard. Today, virtually every web design tool, word processor template, and prototyping application uses it.

A Common Misconception

Many people assume Lorem Ipsum is just random gibberish. While it is garbled and doesn't make coherent sense in Latin, much of it derives from real Latin words and phrases from Cicero's original text. A Latin scholar, Richard McClintock of Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia, is credited with tracing the text back to its Ciceronian source in the modern era, helping to dispel the myth that it was entirely meaningless.

So in short, Lorem Ipsum went from a serious Roman philosophical treatise, to a printer's dummy text, to an icon of modern graphic and web design — a journey of over two thousand years.
